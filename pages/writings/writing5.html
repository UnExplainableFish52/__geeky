  • Solo Levelling: Some people claim that this is an awesome anime while some completely disagree, why people like it? Simple! the writer is very smart and is taking advantage of this generation, by writing the story in a way that every gamer kid tries to relate it to their life and makes it popular in reutrn. A character who is weak, poor and definitely not handsome gets picked as a player in a weird real life game like system. here we can clearly see that the writer is trying to get this relatable to us by making the character initially poor, weak and bad looking, and when this character figures out he is the only one with this solo developing game like system and this system can help him grow stronger, sharper and better, innocent kids are psychologically triggered and they feel like they are the main character and even start to assume watching it makes them stronger and they are actually making progress in their real life.
    Let's decode it together! People who are below 90 Percentile in the competition are not competitive and they find excues, often lazy and NOT SMART, they struggle with things and are barely keeping up, they have lots of bad habits like cheating, no dedication, no hardwork plus no smart work but living in an illusion, they seek instant and temporary pleasure
    How can one become like them then? Firstly, we need to think how is the main character different from others? does he put in different efforts than others? Yes! the main character has suffered a near death experience, was often bullied, has discipline, dedication and faith in himself, you need to believe in yourself and gather enough courage to tackle all the hinderances in your journey, this is a very obvious and simple approach to success that we all know but can not do. So, let us take the harder path, let us make difficult decisions to get stronger, because luck is not reliable and that is the only way we are left with.
