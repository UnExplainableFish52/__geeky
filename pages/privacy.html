Privacy Policy

Fisholophy is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we keep it safe.

Effective: May 1, 2025 Last reviewed: October 19, 2025

1. Introduction

Fisholophy is an educational platform where we share philosophical insights about life, principles, and key understandings. Our aim is to enlighten and inspire our users through thoughtful content.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms described here, please refrain from using our Services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal personal information and strive to respect your privacy. The information we gather falls into these categories:

2.1 Information You Provide

When you submit information through our contact forms, we collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Message content

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

We use Cloudflare Analytics to capture basic usage details, including:

  • Visitor count and session metrics
  • Country or region of origin
  • Access date and time
  • Technical performance indicators

2.3 Cookies and Tracking

Our Services do not use cookies or other tracking technologies beyond what is provided by Cloudflare Analytics for essential site performance reporting.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect helps us deliver and improve Fisholophy. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Respond to your inquiries, comments, or questions
  • Provide feedback, guidance, and support
  • Analyze engagement to refine our content and services
  • Maintain and optimize the performance of our platform

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. We only share data in the following scenarios:

  • Service Providers: Third parties who support our Services (e.g., Cloudflare for analytics, Niotron.com for application development) may process limited data on our behalf.
  • Legal Requirements: Information may be disclosed if required by law or in response to valid requests from public authorities.

5. Data Retention

Contact form submissions are stored indefinitely unless you request deletion. Analytics data follows Cloudflare's retention policies.

6. Third-Party Services

Our Services rely on a small set of third-party tools:

  • Cloudflare Analytics: Provides essential website analytics. Review their policy at https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/.
  • Niotron: Supports application development workflows. Refer to Niotron's published privacy policy for more details.

If we introduce advertisements or additional integrations, this Privacy Policy will be updated to reflect those changes.

7. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to control the personal information we hold about you. Specifically, you may request to:

  • Access the personal information stored about you.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Delete your personal data from our records.
  • Object to specific types of data processing.

To exercise these rights, email us at contacttheidealdev52@gmail.com.

8. Children's Privacy

Fisholophy is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us so we can remove it.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries outside your residence. These locations may have different data protection laws; by using our Services, you consent to these transfers.

10. GDPR and CCPA Compliance

Fisholophy adheres to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users in the European Economic Area and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) for California residents. The rights granted under these regulations are covered in the section above.

11. Updates to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy periodically. When updates occur, we will post the revised policy on this page and adjust the review date. Please check back periodically to stay informed.
